Our Journey

1992

It all began when Saksham Goyal gave wings to AutoWheel, an exclusive service outlet for Kawasaki and Kinetic Honda two wheelers.

1995

AutoWheel had a breakthrough with the self start mechanism as seen in 100 cc 2- stroke bikes, and received accolades and acknowledgement from several automotive magazines then such as Auto India, Car & Bike International, etc.

1997

Kinetic Honda Motor Company approached AutoWheel for their alliance, to be an authorised service partner for their products

2000

Brought forth the revolutionary 24x7 breakdown helpline, which aided customers with break down assistance night and day, all through the yearlong.

2002

Introduced Rent-a-scooter- a then-novel concept which involved two wheeler rentals. This was enthusiastically supported by HAL (Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd), with whom to the present day we keep wonderful ties with. Presently, we cater to several corporate two wheeler mobility solutions..

2004

Unicorn Auto India had another breakthrough in the automotive realms when with all the information and experience gathered, a new in-house software was developed to exclusively manage all operations regarding the sales and service of two wheelers.

2007

Kinetic Honda India acknowledged and appreciated AutoWheel’s unique inventory management software solution, and recommended the same to other service branches.

2010

Kinetic Honda gives AutoWheel their prestigious dealership network that spanned the whole of Bangalore East.

2013

Post the takeover by Mahindra 2 wheelers enterprise, AutoWheel has retained their status as a premier Kinetic vehicle dealership.

2015

Emphasizing their focus solely on two wheeler rentals, AutoWheel discontinued their service activities and networks.

2017

Branded in our new Avatar called Soul-o-ride and floated over as Armaan Auto Sports India Pvt Ltd, we’re here to make a move- quite literally!
